# Development Roadmap

## Milestone 0 — Preserve the concept

- Keep this repository private.
- Maintain AGENTS.md, PRODUCT_VISION.md, and DECISIONS.md.
- Record material decisions before coding them.
- Use GitHub issues for individual features and bugs.

## Milestone 1 — Functional customer prototype

- Refill product selection
- Brought-from-home tare entry
- Preloaded Common Good glass
- Saved-container selection
- Net weight and price calculation
- Pantry Ready catalog
- Glass catalog
- Combined cart
- Cash, check, and Venmo instruction screens
- Reset/new-sale flow
- Responsive tablet layout

## Milestone 2 — Real application foundation

Suggested evaluation stack:
- Next.js/React
- TypeScript
- PostgreSQL/Supabase or equivalent
- Secure authentication
- Hosted deployment
- Automated testing

Deliverables:
- Database schema
- Role-based access
- Persistent products and carts
- Secure settings
- Audit trail for sales edits

## Milestone 3 — Product Workroom

- Product Notebook
- Ingredient and packaging records
- Recipe/formula entry
- Batch scaling
- COGS
- Retail/refill/wholesale pricing
- Supplier links
- Label and asset storage
- Revision history

## Milestone 4 — Inventory and production

- Raw materials
- Packaging
- Bulk/refill stock
- Pantry Ready stock
- Glass stock
- Reorder points
- Batch manufacturing records
- Lot tracking
- Market allocations
- Production recommendations

## Milestone 5 — Hardware

- Supported scale integration

- Product and jar scanning

## Milestone 6 — AI Marketing Studio

- Brand Brain
